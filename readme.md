**My BKpad is my first project based on the model of the boykisser!**

This pad makes your communications easier!
You can express yourself with your emojis, there will be firstly UwU, OwO (4 different buttons)
Then you can express you reflexions by petting his ears! Say hmm or ohh and the longer you keep pressing, the more letters there will have! 
And because everything is not restricted in messages, you will ba able to change your volume and your mic sensibility (with the help of 2 potentiometers :3)

Visuals indicators:
An oled display (showing his mouth) will shurely tell you your mic volume (he can show other emotions when you press)
Twelve leds will show you various informations and interactions :o
